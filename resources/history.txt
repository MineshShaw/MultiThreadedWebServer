Toyota AE86 History
-------------------
The Toyota AE86 was produced from 1983 to 1987 as part of the Corolla Levin and Sprinter Trueno lineup.
Its lightweight chassis, rear-wheel drive, and high-revving engine made it popular in motorsports and drifting.
The AE86 gained global fame through Japanese drifting culture, rally racing, and its starring role in the manga and anime series Initial D.
Even decades later, the AE86 remains a cult classic for car enthusiasts worldwide.
